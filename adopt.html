Adoption FAQs

Do adopted cats come with a health guarantee?

Yes! All our cats are health-checked by a vet. We provide a 30-day health guarantee and are available for any questions.

Can I return a cat if it doesn't work out?

We want all adoptions to be permanent, but we understand life happens. We're here to help and can take the cat back if needed.

Are your cats good with children/other pets?

Each cat's personality and compatibility is listed in their profile. We'll help match you with the perfect fit for your home.
